The curry that will change how you think about curry.

Description

The curry that will transform your perspective on what exactly a curry is. A delicate blend of heat, aromatics and a slap of coconut that will make your palette sing a vibrato.

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Chilli
  • Chicken
  • Garam masala
  • Rogan josh spice blend
  • Tomatoes
  • Coconut milk
  • Rice & Naan

Steps to recreate this heavenly bliss

  1. Heat the oil in a large frying pan over a medium heat. Add the onion and a generous pinch of salt and fry for 8-10 mins, or until the onion has turned golden brown and sticky. Add the garlic and ginger, cooking for a further minute.
  2. Add the chicken to the pan and fry till deliciously golden.
  3. Add the chilli and spice blend and stir for a minute while the aromatics are released.
  4. Liberally throw in the tomatoes and coconut milk, simmer for twenty minutes before finishing by adding the garam masala and coriander and stirring through.
  5. Enthusiastically serve with the magically prepared rice and naan.
  6. Enjoy.
