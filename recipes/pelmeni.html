Lazy pelmeni.

An apt description.

In this recipe, you'll learn how to make these Russian dumplings by hand (mostly).

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Ground meat
  • Onion
  • Pepper
  • Sour cream

Steps to recreate this meat filled morsel

  1. Sift the flour and salt into a large mixing bowl. Add the eggs and water and mix until incorporated.
  2. Knead the dough into has a smooth surface
  3. In a seperate bowl, mix the ground meat, onion, salt, and pepper with your hands until well-combined.
  4. Divide the dough in half and roll out into a thin sheet. Spoon the meat mixture on top of one of the sheets of pastry leaving a gap between them as you go along.
  5. Place the other half of the pastry on top and cut around the filled pastry segments.
  6. Throw your flavour bombs into boiling salt water till they begin to float before consuming with the sour cream.
